Moose: In Loving Memory

This painting of Abbie's dog took about four hours to make. One of the hardest parts in the making of this piece was that Abbie only knew Moose when she was young so I had to go off her memories of what he looked like. I found reference pictures and we decided that he looked a lot like a collie so I tried my best to replicate the anatomy of a collie in a cartoonish style.

Speaking of Abbie...

I don't think this piece needs too much of an explanation. The inspiration behind it was that I wanted to attempt a portrait and Abbie said, "hey you should draw me!" So, like any good friend would, I drew her. The final piece took around 2 hours and 20 minutes. I think it turned out pretty good and my favorite part is definitely the hair.
